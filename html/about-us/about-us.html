About Us

WHO WE ARE?

The Coffee House offers a unique coffee shop atmosphere unlike any other in Dublin. We're not just a place where you can walk in and have your morning cup of coffee (although you're more than welcome to do that), we're a place where you can sit down and enjoy a cup of coffee made to order. If you need to work, we have a lounge area just for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft and cozy seating area that is perfect for your tired mind and body. We offer a wide selection of delicious coffee prepared by our professionally trained baristas. We have only the most demanding, classic and at the same time simple and affordable selection of coffee. All our coffee supplements are produced in-house using natural ingredients (without chemicals and preservatives), which guarantees you the highest taste and health. You can complement your coffee with one of our delicious cakes made by our in-house baker. We are waiting for you in the Coffee House.
